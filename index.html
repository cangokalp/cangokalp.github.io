About Me

Hi! My name is Can Gokalp, and I am a Ph.D. student studying Operations Research at the University of Texas at Austin. I have been working on Optimization Models for three years now. I submitted my first paper to the most esteemed journal in the field of Optimization, Operations Research Journal, on May 6th, 2017. As for computer skills, I am proficient in Python and Matlab. I am also familiar with Mongodb, which I use for an entrepreneurial project. Besides optimization, I am very passionate about learning new techniques to stay on the cutting edge. I have strong interest in Machine Learning and Data Visualization. To this end, I often research learning-algorithms from the web, and follow technology news. I love tech and want to enter the industry upon graduating.

Outside of career pursuits, I enjoy balancing time on the computer with sports. I played college soccer in Turkey for 4 years, and I now play in the Austin Co-ed Soccer League and the intramural tournaments at UT Austin. When I watch sports, it's likely to be the Turkish soccer team Galatasaray, or the Gators and Jacksonville Jaguars for football (I was converted while earning my MS degree at the University of Florida — go Gators!)
